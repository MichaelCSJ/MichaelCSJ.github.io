Seokjun Choi

I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science and Engineering Department at Pohang University of Science and Technology (POSTECH), researching as a member of the Computer Graphics Lab, advised by Prof. Seung-Hwan Baek.

My research interest primarily focuses on computer graphics and computational imaging, with a focus on photometric stereo, inverse rendering, and computational illumination.
